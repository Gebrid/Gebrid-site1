Unlimited Approvals

Route Deviation
Execution paths outside strategy parameters trigger automatic hold. Capital moves only through verified routes.
Permission Escalation
Contracts requesting access beyond declared intent are flagged and quarantined — before any approval is granted.
Oracle Sensitivity
Price feed anomalies mid-execution halt affected routes pending re-evaluation. Oracle manipulation is surface-scanned at every step.
Policy Breach
Any action outside the operator's configured policy envelope is blocked at the gate — not rolled back after. Execution is prevented, not undone.

Execution Modes

Adaptive risk posture across market conditions

● NORMAL
Full execution. All agents active. Standard policy thresholds apply.
● RESTRICTED
Reduced position limits. High-risk routes suspended. Manual review required for large actions.
● CRISIS
Execution halted. Capital protection mode active. Only guardian operations permitted.
  • Mode transitions triggered by on-chain signals or operator policy override
  • All mode changes are logged with timestamp, trigger, and approving role
  • No single agent or strategy can force a mode downgrade without governance approval
